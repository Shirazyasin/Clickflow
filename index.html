FAQ

Questions we get on every first call.

Do we need to be on Klaviyo already?

No. If you're on Mailchimp, Attentive or something else, migrating is part of week one — list, segments, templates and historical data. If you're not on any platform yet, we'll get you set up and integrated with Shopify before we build anything.

How big does our list need to be?

Most brands we work with are doing £1m–£15m a year with a list somewhere north of 20,000. Below that, a retainer usually costs more than it returns, and we'll say so on the call rather than take the money.

Who writes the copy and does the design?

We do, both. You give us the brand guidelines, product info and anything you're launching. You get the calendar and the designs to approve before anything sends.

Is the Klaviyo subscription included?

No — you pay Klaviyo directly for your plan, and it stays in your name. The account is yours. If you ever leave, everything we built stays with you.

How long until we see anything?

First flows are live in week two, first campaign in week four. Meaningful revenue shifts usually show in month two, once the flows have had a full cycle of traffic through them.

What do you need from us each month?

About thirty minutes. Approve the calendar, tell us what's launching, send product images. That's the job.
